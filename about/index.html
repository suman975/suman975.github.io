About

I’m a Pokhara-based artist from Nepal, working mainly with digital painting and pen sketches. My practice includes portraits, as well as building and landscape drawings, with a focus on expression, mood, and visual storytelling. This website showcases a selection of my ongoing work and personal explorations.
